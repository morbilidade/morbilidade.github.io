Instructions for use

The tool MORBIDITY can be consulted in the second tab of this page or through the link morbilidade.github.io/en/morbidity.



Search options

The tool allows the information to be consulted according to the following options:

Analysis level:

  • Level 1: Components (groups of diseases)
  • Level 2: Classes (combinations of diseases within groups)

Geographical region:

  • Portugal
  • Each of the seven NUTS II:
    1. North
    2. Center
    3. Lisbon
    4. Alentejo
    5. Algarve
    6. Azores
    7. Madeira

Morbidity indicator (considered in the model as an outcome):

  1. Negative self-perceived general health
  2. Absence from work (in employed individuals)
  3. Physical functional limitations
  4. Personal care activities limitations (>65 years)
  5. Household activities limitations (>65 years)
  6. Intensity of bodily pain
  7. Impact of the pain on daily life
  8. Hospitalisation as inpatient
  9. Hospitalisation as day patient
  10. Consultation of a general practitioner
  11. Consultation of other specialist
  12. Use of medicines prescribed
  13. Use of medicines not prescribed
  14. Out-of-pocket health expenditure

Graphic presentation of results adjusted for:

  • Confounders and components
  • Only confounders

Graphic representation

The results are presented graphically in a representation similar to a slopegraph / arrow diagram, comparing the hierarchy of the components or classes for the calculated "individual" association measure (prevalence ratio) and the "populational" impact measure (population attributable fraction).

The ranking to represent graphically can also be selected:



Table representation

All calculated results for the selected options are also available in table format.
